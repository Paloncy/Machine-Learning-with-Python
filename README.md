Heart disease is seen to be caused by the narrowing of the blood vessels thereby causing lesser amount of blood and oxygen to the heart (Song, Chen and Antoniou, 2021). 
The reasons for a major cause of deaths like the heart disease has been studied and has been discovered that there are many pre-dominant features that could lead to a heart disease. As a machine learning engineer, I can aim to use my model to predict the people that are more susceptible to a heart disease depending on their features, however, this cannot be used independently on its own but in conjunction with the diagnosis of a medical practitioner.
For a lethal killer like the heart disease, early detection has a major role to play in survival and leading a normal life. A dataset from the CDC will be used to train and test the machine learning algorithm and give an accuracy score afterwards. 
For this machine learning model, we will be using Logistic regression, Random forest and Naïve bayes algorithms to test the model.
